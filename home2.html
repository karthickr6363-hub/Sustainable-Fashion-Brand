Brand Milestones

2018

The Beginning

EcoLux was born in a small studio with a big dream: to create beautiful fashion that doesn't cost the earth.

2019

First Organic Collection

Launched our first 100% organic cotton collection, partnering with certified fair-trade farms in India.

2020

Carbon Neutral Status

Achieved carbon neutral certification and launched our innovative recycling program.

2021

Global Expansion

Expanded to 15 countries, bringing sustainable fashion to conscious consumers worldwide.

2024

Impact Leader

Recognized as the most impactful sustainable fashion brand, saving over 10 million liters of water.
