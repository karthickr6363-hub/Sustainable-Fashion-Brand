About EcoLux

Pioneering sustainable luxury fashion that honors both people and planet

Our Story

Born from a simple question: "Can fashion be both beautiful and responsible?"

EcoLux began in 2018 in a small Portland studio with a revolutionary idea: luxury fashion doesn't have to cost the earth. Our founder, Sarah Chen, a former fashion industry insider, witnessed firsthand the environmental and social costs of fast fashion and knew there had to be a better way.

What started as a small collection of organic cotton dresses has grown into a global movement. Today, we partner with over 500 artisans across 15 countries, creating timeless pieces that respect traditional craftsmanship while embracing sustainable innovation.

Our Values

Sustainability First

Every decision begins with its environmental impact. We continuously challenge ourselves to reduce our footprint and innovate for a greener future.

Radical Transparency

We believe customers have the right to know exactly where their clothes come from. Our supply chain is an open book.

Uncompromising Quality

Luxury means exceptional quality that lasts. We never cut corners on materials, craftsmanship, or attention to detail.

Cultural Respect

We honor and preserve traditional craftsmanship while providing fair economic opportunities for artisan communities worldwide.

Circular Thinking

We design for circularity from the start, ensuring our products can be reused, recycled, or returned to the earth safely.

Innovation Leadership

We push boundaries in sustainable fashion technology, materials science, and business models for positive impact.
